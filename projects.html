Weathosaurus

Inspired by Aurorasaurus, Weathosaurus is a pure HTML/CSS/JS application that extracts data from OpenWeatherMap API. A javascript station model is also constructed based on extracted meteorological data, although not performing up to maximum percision.


This was made in collaboration with my Science Olympiad Meteorlogy partner (@bdcl)!









AI Song Covers

Currently, I do not know how to train my own AI voice models! Therefore, I experimented with some online pre-trained data. Below is the result!


My attempt at singing "Yue Guang":

"AI Jennie Kim" singing "Yue Guang" (bad), based on my voice:



It's not great at all (I think I sound better than the AI), but at least I got to explore the training process (on Google Colab).


Based on my understanding, ContentVec is downloaded: this self-supervised learning (SSL) model disentangles speakers without much unwanted variations from the content. A PyTorch file is thendownloaded, with so-vits-svc (Singing Voice Conversion) 4.0 cloned into local empty directory. I pasted a pre-made AI voice url from MEGA (Google Drive, MEGA zip files compatiable), and uploaded my singing voice to Google Colab Files. The model then converted my voice to the targeted AI voice.
